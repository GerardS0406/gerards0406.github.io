Group Projects

The projects below show off some of my work done with others.

  • Office Robbery

    You're a thief with a gun. You enter an office building at night filled with important documents, your goal is to steal them before the security guard makes his rounds. Created for an assignment in one day.

  • Fly Away Penguin

    iOS game created when I was 12 years old. No longer available. Flappy Bird clone written in XCloud.

  • T6 Cold War Mod

    A mod for Call of Duty Black Ops II that implements the Cold War Zombies elements. These elements include but not limited to, a weapon tier system, an armor system, all new health system, and much more.

  • T8 Survival Maps Mod

    Similar to the survival maps created in Black Ops II Zombies, these survival maps are available in Call of Duty Black Ops 4 Zombies. Adds a survival maps location on each main map. Wonder weapons available in the mystery box.

  • T6 Bots Mod

    A mod for Call of Duty Black Ops II that add bots into the zombies mode. The bots will shoot, revive, buy weapons, pack a punch. Still a WIP.

  • Upcoming Game

    A game being developed using the Unity Game Engine over the summer. Stay tuned.
