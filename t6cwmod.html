T6 Cold War Mod

Solo Project

  • Engine: Call of Duty: Black Ops II Mod Tools
  • Development Time: Ongoing (~6 months off and on)
  • Role: Modder & Programmer

Features and Focus

  • Enhanced Zombies HUD with dynamic floating scores.
  • Custom animations ported from Black Ops 1.
  • Automated material generation for models and textures.
  • Support for looping video backgrounds using .webm files.

Overview

This project aims to bring modern innovations to the Zombies mode of Black Ops 2. The goal is to add fresh gameplay elements while maintaining the nostalgic experience of the original game.

From HUD customizations to advanced animations and video integration, this mod adds new layers of polish to an already beloved game. Through scripting, material automation, and Lua tweaks, the experience becomes smoother for both players and modders alike.
